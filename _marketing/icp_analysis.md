# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Analysis
## Overview
Based on real-world pain points extracted from industry research, sports technology adoption is currently bottlenecked by the "Collaboration Conundrum" (too many siloed apps) and "Data Overload" (too many metrics, too little actionable insight). 

SporlyWorks Pro Suite is perfectly positioned to solve this by consolidating workflows safely into a localized, unified suite.

## The Top 3 Addressable Segments

### 1. The Overburdened Small/Mid-Sized High School Athletic Director
*   **Persona:** Typically a former coach who got promoted. Handles budgets, logistics, compliance, and parent communication for 20+ teams.
*   **Budget:** $5,000 - $15,000 annually for all department software. Extremely conscious of recurring per-seat licensing.
*   **Pain Point:** Fragmented tools. They use separate tools for video, messaging, and scheduling. It wastes time and money.
*   **SporlyWorks Value:** The Pro Suite offers flat-rate consolidation. One login to manage 88 extensions securely without heavy IT overhead.

### 2. Independent/Private Elite Performance Coaches
*   **Persona:** Runs a specialized training facility or mobile business. Trains D-I bound high schoolers or off-season pros.
*   **Budget:** High willingness to pay ($2k - $5k MRR per facility) but demands immediate ROI and "premium aesthetics."
*   **Pain Point:** They need to look incredibly professional to justify high client fees, but lack the resources for custom enterprise software. They get bogged down parsing complex sensor data.
*   **SporlyWorks Value:** Professional, gorgeous UI right out of the box with the advanced analytics tools natively built-in (e.g., our physics and movement extensions). 

### 3. D-II / D-III College Assistant Coaches (The "Grinders")
*   **Persona:** Responsible for recruiting, scouting, and player development. Wears many hats and works 80 hours a week.
*   **Budget:** Department budget is tight; often pays for software out of pocket or discretionary recruiting funds ($1k - $3k/yr).
*   **Pain Point:** Overworked. They cannot afford the $100k+ D-I enterprise platforms (like Catapult or Teamworks), so they cobble together Google Sheets and generic apps.
*   **SporlyWorks Value:** A secure, enterprise-grade suite at a fraction of the cost, eliminating manual data entry and unifying their scouting/recruiting workflows.
