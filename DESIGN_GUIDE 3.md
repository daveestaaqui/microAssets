# SporlyWorks Brand Identity: The San Francisco Monoline Mandate

## Core Philosophy
We build high-end, minimalist utility extensions. Our brand is professional, sophisticated, and memorable. We avoid generic, colorful, or overly complex designs.

## Visual Identity (The "SF Mushroom" Style)
- **Palette**: 
  - `Charcoal` (#1A1A1B) - Base / Text
  - `Cream` (#F5F5F0) - Background / Accents
  - `Spore Gold` (#D4AF37) - Subtle Highlights (Use sparingly)
- **Motif**: 
  - A subtle mushroom or spore integration. 
  - Never a "cartoon" mushroom. 
  - Think monoline geometric shapes where the "cap" of the mushroom forms part of the utility icon (e.g., a magnifying glass where the handle is a stem).
- **Typography**: 
  - Clean, geometric sans-serif (Inter, Outfit, or Roboto).
  - High letter-spacing for headers.
- **Iconography**:
  - Absolute minimalist monoline.
  - Consistent stroke width (2px-3px).
  - High negative space usage.

## Design Rules for Agents
1. **No Gradients**: Use solid charcoal/cream or very subtle flat transitions.
2. **Icon Integrity**: Icons must be sharp at 16x16px (Chrome Toolbar).
3. **Negative Space**: Use whitespace to denote premium quality.
4. **Consistency**: Every extension must feel like it came from the same studio.

## Examples
- **OmniSearch**: A magnifying glass with a monoline mushroom stem handle.
- **LinkGuard**: A shield icon where the center negative space forms a spore.
- **ManySignals**: A candlestick chart where the wicks have tiny circular "spore" caps.
