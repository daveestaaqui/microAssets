## ⚙️ GitHub Pages Deployment Instructions

### Quick Setup (Manual - Takes 30 seconds):

1. Go to your repository: `https://github.com/daveestaaqui/microAssets`
2. Click **Settings** tab
3. Scroll down and click **Pages** (left sidebar)
4. Under "Build and deployment" → **Source** dropdown
5. Select **Deploy from a branch**
6. **Branch:** `main`
7. **Folder:** `/ (root)`
8. Click **Save**

GitHub will build automatically. Your site will be live at:
`https://daveestaaqui.github.io/microAssets/`

**OR** if you have a custom domain (`sporlyworks.com`):
1. In GitHub Pages settings, scroll to **Custom domain**
2. Enter: `sporlyworks.com`
3. Follow DNS propagation instructions
